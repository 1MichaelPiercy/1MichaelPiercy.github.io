Overview

The purpose of the application will be to rent a van. It will include multiple pages that can show off the vans, gather payment info, pickup location, etc. With each page helping to target different people. These people will mostly consist of moving of any sorts and DIYers or business owners, but mostly people that just need a van in general. It will also highlight other vans that they could use to achieve what they need.


Homepage

For right now the homepage will serve as a hub. It will attract the attention of anyone in need of renting a van. The user will also be able to see a navigation menu that will help them go from one part of the website to another. It will also have a form section allowing for the customer to sign up for a monthly email from the company for promotions. There does seem to be a plan to where you can also access other parts of the website through clicking the pictures and menu. No data will be collected aside from getting data for location (this may change). We will not need to get data validation on this page at all.

Pricing and Availability

The main purpose of this page will be to collect data from the customer in the form of payments. This will be done through form elements. It will also allow them to schedule when to pick up the vehicle of their choosing and how long they want to keep it. There will be data validation to make sure its the right info and anything that needs to be filled out. It will not have any hyperlinks aside to a webpage showing reviews for the company. The way the availability will be chosen will be through a drop down menu allowing them to select how long they need the van. Most likely showing it through days, weeks, and months. Once this is all done and submitted successfully, it will "delete" everything on the page and show the results.

Story of Van Rental

This page is solely meant to give the audience an idea of how the company was founded and what the goal of the founder is. It will not contain anything else aside from whats needed like pictures or collage of somesort. It is meant for anyone that maybe neaver heard nor seen the company around before. It will not collect any sort of data from the user. It may end up having interactive pictures in one way or another. It may also contain some sort of hyperlink to the vans portion of the page and a google search of images showing vans.

Vans

This part of the website is specifically meant to show the vans to the customer. It will have pictures of the vans that the customer can select. There will be a hyperlink at the bottom directing them to paymeant. It will also contain a description of the van such as what it will be best suited for, items inside, engine size, etc. There is not much else planned other than links directing the customer to payment.

Store Locations

A drop down menu with a picture of a map showing the locations of the stores will pop up. The customer can select where to pick up the van at by clicking on it through the drop down menu. Once this process is completed, a prompt will pop up in order to make sure the customer selected the right location they need to pick up the van at. The customer will not have to share any data about their location to achieve this. Text will also appear below the drop down menu saying what the customer picked. If they dicide to change locations, they can. The previous information will be erased and replaced.

Van Reviews

Reviews of the company and its vans will be shown on this webpage. It will contain a table like format seperating each review with a button at the bottom to click on that allows more reviews to become available. Not sure if it will be drop down or go from page 1 to page 2 type deal yet. Only data that will be collected is first name and general location of the customer. The date of which it was published will automatically show up. A user will be able to hover over the review in order to enlarge it and get a better view of it.

Functionality of the Website

We will have a somewhat basic calculator. What it will do is add up the total including the tax of the sale. We will also have a couple of areas for user input, like ordering the van, payment, replacement of images and text through the use of JavaScript. We will also use a special drop down menu that calls upon an array for the location while in use.>
